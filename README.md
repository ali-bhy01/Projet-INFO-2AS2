# ASRS — Advanced School Run Strategy (DAX 40)

Quantitative backtest of Tom Hougaard's "School Run Strategy" on DAX 40 Futures.  
20 years of data (2006–2026) · 5-min bars · 3 layers of improvement

---

## Strategy in one sentence

After the 4th five-minute candle of the DAX session (09:15 CET), place a buy-stop above its high and a sell-stop below its low. Let the winner run to end of day. No profit target.

## Final strategy rules (3 filters total)

1. **Signal bar** = 4th 5-min candle (09:15 CET open). Range must be between 10 and 55 pts.
2. **Entry** = Buy-stop at signal_high + 2 / Sell-stop at signal_low − 2 (OCO)
3. **Stop loss** = opposite entry price (signal range + 4 pts risk)
4. **Exit** = Stop hit OR end of day at 17:30 CET — no profit target
5. **Skip** Fridays, January, July, August
